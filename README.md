# Notion Avatar Marker

An online tool for making notion-style avatars.

I18n supported:

- [English](https://notion-avatar.vercel.app/en)
- [简体中文](https://notion-avatar.vercel.app/zh)

## Styles

- 😉 11 face shapes
- 👃🏼 11 noses
- 👄 11 mouths
- 👀 11 eyes
- 👁️ 11 eyebrows
- 🕶️ 10 glasses
- 💇‍♀️ 30 hairstyles
- 🎅🏼 10 beards
- 💋 10 facial details
- 💍 10 accessories
